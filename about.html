About Esraa Gallery

A handmade brand shaped by beauty, warmth, and meaningful creation.

Esraa Gallery is a premium handmade brand built around resin art, candles, decor, personalized gifts, and custom event pieces designed to make moments feel elevated, intimate, and unforgettable.

Started with purpose Created during the COVID pandemic as a creative outlet and source of beauty.
Grown with intention Developed into a premium handmade brand with strong identity and refined presentation.
Made for meaning Designed for gifting, celebrations, keepsakes, and the moments people remember most.

Our Beginning

What began during COVID as a creative outlet became something much bigger.

Esraa Gallery began during the COVID pandemic — a time when so many people were searching for comfort, purpose, and small ways to create beauty in uncertain days. What started as a personal creative outlet became a meaningful practice rooted in patience, experimentation, and the joy of making things by hand.

Over time, that creative spark evolved into a refined handmade brand. The work became more intentional, the visual direction became more premium, and each piece began to carry a clearer purpose: to help people celebrate, gift, remember, and create beauty around meaningful moments.

Today, Esraa Gallery stands as a handmade brand with a soft-luxury feel — warm, elegant, editorial, and deeply personal.

Our Process

How each piece moves from idea to finished handmade creation.

01

Concept & Inspiration

A piece often starts with a mood, a purpose, a celebration, or a feeling that should come through visually.

02

Material Pairing

Colors, textures, resin styling, candle presentation, and decorative direction are chosen to fit the final emotional and visual tone.

03

Handmade Production

Why the Products Matter

These pieces are made to become part of real memories and meaningful occasions.

Esraa Gallery products are designed to live beyond the moment of purchase. A tray may become part of a wedding setup. A candle may be gifted to mark a new chapter. A personalized piece may sit in someone’s home and carry emotional meaning every time it is seen.

That is why the brand focuses on more than product categories. It focuses on purpose: helping people celebrate, honor, gift, decorate, and express care in a way that feels elevated and genuine.
