Our Story

Born during COVID, grown through creativity, shaped into a premium handmade brand.

Esraa Gallery began during the COVID pandemic as a creative outlet — a way to keep making beauty in uncertain times. What started as a personal craft journey grew into a refined handmade brand focused on warmth, craftsmanship, and emotionally meaningful design.

Today, the brand creates premium resin art, candles, decor, personalized gifts, and custom event pieces that help people celebrate moments, honor memories, and gift with intention.
