Policies

Clear expectations for ordering from a premium handmade brand.

Policy 01

Shipping Policy

Shipping timelines vary depending on product type, season, and whether the item is a ready-to-ship piece or a handmade-to-order item. Customers should allow additional handling time for custom pieces, event requests, and detailed handmade orders.

Once an order is prepared for shipment, tracking details may be shared through the future live order system or confirmation email flow. Shipping delays caused by carriers, weather, customs, or incorrect address submission are outside direct brand control.

Policy 02

Returns Policy

Because Esraa Gallery focuses on handmade items, personalized creations, and custom event work, returns may be limited depending on the nature of the order. Customized, personalized, and made-for-event items are generally considered final sale unless a confirmed issue occurred during fulfillment.

For non-custom eligible pieces, return requests should be submitted within a clearly defined period in the future live store policy setup. Returned items must be unused, in original condition, and packaged appropriately.

Policy 03

Damaged Orders Policy

If an order arrives damaged, the customer should contact Esraa Gallery as soon as possible with the order reference, clear photos of the item, and photos of the packaging condition. This helps verify what happened and determine the most appropriate resolution.

Approved cases may be handled through replacement, repair discussion, partial resolution, or another appropriate support path depending on product type, availability, and the extent of the issue.

Policy 04

Event Orders Policy

Weddings, graduations, baby showers, gifting batches, and other event-based orders should be placed as early as possible. Large quantities, detailed personalization, coordinated presentation, and shipping windows require planning time.

Event orders may involve additional approval steps such as quantity confirmation, design direction, personalization review, timeline approval, and custom pricing. Rush requests may not always be available depending on production capacity.

Policy 05

Processing Time Policy

Esraa Gallery is a handmade brand, which means many pieces require time for design, pouring, setting, finishing, packaging, and quality review. Processing time can vary by product type and order complexity.

Ready-made items may move faster, while custom and personalized orders often require longer timelines. Customers should plan ahead for gifts, events, and important deadlines whenever possible.
